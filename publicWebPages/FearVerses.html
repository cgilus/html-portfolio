Bible verses for Fear and Axiety

Be Brave


1). Isaiah 41:10 -- Fear thou not; for I am with thee: be not dismaybed; for I am thy God: I will strengthen thee;
yea, I will help thee; yea, I will uphold thee with the right hand of my righteousness

2). 2 Timothy 1:7 -- For God hath not give us a spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind.

3). Palms 23:4 -- Yea,though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil:
for thou art with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.
