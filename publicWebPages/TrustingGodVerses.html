Bible verses for Trusting God

Trusting in God


1). Proverbs 3:5 -7 -- Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; lean not unto thine own understanding.
In all thy ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct thy paths.
Be not wise in thine own eyes: fear the LORD, and depart from evil.

2). Isaiah 26:3,4 -- Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.
Trust ye in the LORD forever: for in the LORD JEHOVAH is everlasting strength.

3). Palms 56:3 -- What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.
